Tap 30

A Simple game that requires no internet that is 100% free of ads for your full enjoyment. The concept is easy to understand, available for all ages. Prevent anyone of the boxes from reaching thirty by taping them, each time you clear a few boxes under five seconds you get a boost! Use your boost wisely; it only would last 5 seconds. Challenge your friends and get creative with game modes! A simple application where you can start doing your own challenges.
